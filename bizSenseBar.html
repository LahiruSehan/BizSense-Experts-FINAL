Complete Feature Set

Every Module Your
Outlet Needs

From stock purchasing to equipment maintenance — everything built in. Turn off what you don't need from Settings.

01

Cash Book

Cash and Bank tracked separately. Every sale, expense, and payment flows through. Running totals and closing position for any date range. Customisable expense categories.

02

Credit Ledger

Every credit sale tracked per customer. Collect against specific invoices. Payments auto-create cash book entries and reduce the outstanding balance.

03

Cheque Book

Track cheques received and issued. Cheque number, bank, party, due date, status — pending, cleared, bounced, or cancelled. Supplier payments and large settlements.

04

Supplier Ledger

Every purchase order and payment per supplier. Know exactly what is owed, to whom, and when. Never miss a supplier payment again.

05

Stock & GRN

Goods Received Notes raise purchase orders, receive stock, and auto-update levels. Sequential GRN numbers. Stock adjustments with reason, notes, and full audit log.

06

Staff Management

4-digit PIN clock-in. Roles — Owner, Manager, Cashier, Bartender. Granular module-level access control. The Owner restricts exactly which screens each staff member can see.

07

Equipment Maintenance

Log jobs for fridges, taps, coffee machines, dishwashers. Technician assigned, parts used, advance paid, repair cost. Status moves Pending → In Progress → Completed.

08

Day-End Closing

Count the drawer, enter the amount, see the exact variance. Day-end captures total sales, refunds, expenses, and bottle deposits. Each record stored with staff and notes.

09

Audit Log

Every significant action — price changes, refunds, stock adjustments, day-end closings — logged with staff name, module, action type, and timestamp. Nothing changes silently.

10

Multi-Store Admin Panel

Monitor multiple installations — sales figures, staff lists, activity, billing status per store. Remote store lock for overdue payments with one click from the admin panel.

11

SMS Notifications

Send SMS to customers from within the app — promotions, payment reminders, or any custom message. Full SMS log records every message sent.

12

Full Customisation

Shop details, VAT/NBT rates, currency, modules on/off, receipt layout, auto-print, 9 colour themes — every setting yours to configure. Turn off features you don't need.
