Features No Other POS
in Sri Lanka Has

Not add-ons. Core built-in capabilities designed from day one for the phone retail industry.

01

IMEI & Serial Tracking

Every device tracked from arrival to sale and beyond. IMEI tied to GRN, variant, receipt, and customer record. Includes device blacklist for flagged handsets.

02

Repair Job Management

Log repairs with fault description, assigned technician, and parts used. Track every status from intake to collection. Bill directly from the repair record.

03

Warranty Management

Issue warranties at point of sale. Manage the full lifecycle — record claims, track resolutions, and maintain complete per-device history.

04

EMI Plan Management

Create instalment plans with custom down payments, tenure, and interest. Auto-generate schedules. Track outstanding balances per customer in real time.

05

Device Blacklist

Flag stolen or reported IMEI numbers. Staff get an instant alert when a blacklisted device is scanned — protecting your business from buying hot stock.

06

Technician Assignment

Assign repair jobs to specific technicians. Track workload and individual performance across your workshop team.

07

Parts & Labour Tracking

Parts used in repairs auto-deducted from accessories inventory. Labour tracked separately for true cost and profit per job.

08

SMS Customer Notifications

Auto-send SMS when a repair is ready, a warranty is expiring, or an EMI payment is due. Customers stay informed automatically.
